Domestic Tariff:

Domestic, Multi-tenements, Old age homes, Handlooms comes to this category, they shall pay 4.5 per unit, upto 200 kWh and ₹ 6.00 for the unit consumption 201 to 251. For the consumption of above 250 units, the consumer shall fall into another tariff plan such as, ₹4.5 for the first 200 units, ₹ 6 for the next 50 units, ₹ 8/- for the next 50 units such as 251 to 300, 9 for the next 100 units from 301 to 400, 10/- for the next 100 units from 401 to 500 and the ₹11 for the remaining consumption will be charged. Here there is not fixed charges will be billed for the consumer.
